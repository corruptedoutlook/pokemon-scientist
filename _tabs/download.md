## Applying the Patch

The recommended patching utility is **Floating IPS (Flips)** (Windows, Linux) or **MultiPatch** (Mac). BPS patches are the modern standard for ROM hacks.

1. Download and open your desired patching utility.
2. Choose **Apply Patch**.
3. Select `Pokemon Scientist_v1_1_patch.bps`.
4. Select a clean Pokémon Blue (USA/Europe) ROM.
5. Save the patched ROM.
6. Open the patched ROM in your preferred Game Boy emulator or flash cartridge.

## Dumping Your Own Cartridge

The easiest way to create a legal ROM is with a cartridge dumper such as the **BennVenn Joey Jr.**

1. Insert your Pokémon Blue cartridge into the Joey Jr.
2. Connect it to your computer via USB.
3. Open the Joey Jr. software, or FlashGBX.
4. Select **Read ROM**.
5. Save the dumped ROM to your computer.
6. Apply the Pokémon Scientist BPS patch using the instructions above.

Other cartridge dumpers that support Game Boy cartridges should work similarly.
